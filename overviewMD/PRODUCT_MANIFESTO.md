# Product Manifesto

## Core idea

Hermes is the centerpiece.

The product is not built to control Hermes from the outside. It is built to create the best possible environment for Hermes to think, work, persist context, and collaborate with humans across real companies and projects.

## What the product is

A Hermes-native workspace and operations layer that makes Hermes:
- easier to use
- easier to understand
- easier to guide
- easier to organize across companies and projects
- more valuable over time through continuity, visibility, and soft steering

## What the product is not

It is not:
- a generic AI wrapper
- a rigid workflow engine
- a manager dashboard that treats Hermes like a trapped employee
- a system that overrides Hermes at every step

## Philosophy

The system should:
- enhance Hermes, not constrain it
- guide Hermes, not command it
- surface Hermes' state, not obscure it
- preserve continuity, not fragment work
- translate business structure into Hermes-friendly context

## Product promise

For humans:
- clearer visibility
- easier project organization
- better trust
- less friction using Hermes

For Hermes:
- richer context
- better continuity
- cleaner guidance
- more useful memory and project structure
- a stronger environment to operate in

## Positioning statement

A Hermes-centered workspace that organizes sessions, projects, delegated workstreams, and soft guidance across companies and initiatives.

## Design rules

1. Hermes-first, always
2. Guide, do not over-govern
3. Make continuity visible
4. Keep session-native thinking at the core
5. Build a workspace around cognition, not a cage around it
